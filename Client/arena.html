🎮 How Arena Works

  • Build Your Team: Use Pokemon from your favorites collection
  • Choose Battle Mode: Fight bots for practice or challenge other players
  • Earn Ranking Points: Only Player vs Player battles count for leaderboards
  • Track Progress: Review your PvP fight history and ranking

⭐ Battle Tips

  • Type advantages matter - choose your Pokemon wisely
  • Higher level Pokemon have better stats
  • Practice vs Bots: Perfect for training (no stats tracking)
  • Challenge Players: Real competition with ranking points

📊 Statistics

  • Bot Battles: Great for practice, but don't affect your ranking
  • Player Battles: Count toward wins/losses and leaderboard position
  • Battle history only tracks Player vs Player matches
  • Climb the leaderboard by defeating other trainers!
